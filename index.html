The PEASIS idea

We style the image, so the presence can stun.

The peacock stands for beauty, poise, distinction, and the confidence to be seen. PEASIS carries that idea into fashion: clothing as identity, memory, and presence — not just fabric.

Meet the Head Designer

A lifetime of fashion, rebuilt with purpose.

PEASIS is led by a Lagos-based head designer with over three decades of experience shaping fabric into clothing people can feel proud to wear.

After years of building her craft and running her own shop, PEASIS is a renewed chapter — not a beginning from empty hands, but a return to mastery, care, and the work she never stopped loving.

The PEASIS Experience

How a PEASIS piece comes to life.

Our process is personal because custom fashion should feel considered from the first message to the final statement.

01

Begin with an inquiry

The client reaches out with interest, inspiration, occasion, timeline, or a style direction.

02

Refine the vision

We shape the fit, fabric direction, silhouette, and personal details that make the piece feel intentional.

03

Craft with care

The garment is created with attention to balance, finish, structure, and the designer’s eye.

04

Fit, finish, and elevate

The piece is adjusted and refined so it carries both beauty and confidence.

05

Deliver the final statement

The finished piece becomes a visual expression of image, presence, and memory.
