Meet the Head Designer

A lifetime of fashion, rebuilt with purpose.

PEASIS is guided by a head designer who has spent more than three decades shaping fabric into clothing women can feel proud to wear. Her work carries the patience of experience, the eye of a maker, and the quiet confidence of someone who has lived the craft.

After years of building her fashion journey and running her own shop, PEASIS represents a renewed chapter: a return to the design work she never stopped loving.

The PEASIS Story

Not a new skill. A renewed dream.

PEASIS is not starting from empty hands. It is built from years of measuring, cutting, sewing, fitting, correcting, refining, and creating for real women.

After losing the physical shop she once built, the dream did not disappear. PEASIS is a new expression of that same lifelong craft — more intentional, more visible, and ready to serve women who value clothing with story and care.
